9 FPIC Safeguards

  1. Obtain consent before any credit generation or land activity
  2. Independent verification by qualified third party (not developer)
  3. Documentation in local language(s) — not French-only
  4. Community right to say "no" without penalty
  5. Benefit-sharing agreement signed before project start
  6. Ongoing consent — periodic renewal, not one-time
  7. Grievance mechanism with independent escalation path
  8. Community monitors with access to project data
  9. Exit clause if community withdraws consent

Key Cases (Praezedenzfaelle)

  • Isangi REDD+ — 1.39M credits sold (>USD 7M), community consent obtained 5 years after credit sales. Annulled Jan 2024.
  • Tradelink — 5 concessions annulled (project area exceeded limits)
  • Cambodia REDD+ — 29 communities, no FPIC documented. HRW investigation, forced evictions.
  • Glencore (CH) — CHF 2M + USD 150M fine (Aug 2024) for DRC corruption. Sets Swiss prosecution precedent.
