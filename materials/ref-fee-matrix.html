Revenue Sharing Principle (Umsatzteilung)

  • No default split — silence in contract = MANUFACTURER gets 0%
  • Split specified per Work Order, not at framework level
  • 2 fees shared: Initiation + Participation
  • 3 fees COMPANY-only: Structuring, SaaS, PaaS
