Warranty Chain

MANUFACTURER → COMPANY → CLIENT
Problem: "Original Purchaser" restriction in MANUFACTURER warranty limits pass-through to CLIENT.
Solution: Pass-Through clause (FA Art. 12.4) — COMPANY assigns warranty rights to CLIENT. 5 gaps identified in Draft 0.
Equipment redundancy: Built-in redundancy — single device failure does not break measurement chain (Messkette).
